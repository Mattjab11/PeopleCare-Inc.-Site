Our Mission

Need always appears to be so overwhelming. Yet in our experience, it begins to cower in the face of a simple smile. It flinches when a hand takes an elbow.

Fact is, PeopleCare people, our clients, live with need every day and our volunteers face it down armed with little more than car keys or a telephone or a hot meal.

Using primarily volunteers PeopleCare will assist and support the homebound elderly by providing socialization, emotional support and access to service providers where no one is denied services.

In 1990, PeopleCare began providing a volunteer friendly visitation program to the homebound, which often served as a respite for a primary caregiver. Today, PeopleCare is still reaching out to the elderly, specifically targeting communities without programs to benefit those whose age or physical condition limits their mobility.

How We Serve

PeopleCare offers comprehensive support services to help elderly community members maintain their independence and dignity.

Friendly Visitors

A volunteer friend to visit a homebound older adult to hold a hand, play a game, or just sit and talk.

Transportation

Caring volunteer drivers who focus on the special needs of those who have no other way to get to medical visits, drug store pick-ups and financial appointments.

Voucher Taxi Program

A limited use subsidized taxi program for those who no longer drive but are still independent at their destination.

Information & Referral

Each referral receives attention it deserves, including follow-up to make sure your needs are being met.

Grocery Shopping

Weekly excursions offering access to local groceries, banks and pharmacies with caregiver assistance available.

Caregiver Support

Powerful Tools for Caregivers program designed to provide caregivers with the tools needed to take care of themselves.
