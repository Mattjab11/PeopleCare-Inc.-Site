Volunteer Transportation Programs

A caring volunteer driver who focuses on the special needs of those who have no other way to get to handle personal errands like medical visits, drug store pick-ups and financial appointments in a safe manner.

Voucher Taxi Program

A limited use subsidized taxi program for those who no longer drive but are still independent at their destination. Designed especially for rides to medical and financial appointments, drug store or grocery shopping.
